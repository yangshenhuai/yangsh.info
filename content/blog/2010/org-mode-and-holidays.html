--- title: Org-mode and holidays created: !!timestamp '2010-11-15 16:11:00' image: /media/images/blog/2010/emacs-org-mode-holidays.png tags: - Emacs - Org --- {% block excerpt %} {% mark excerpt %} [Org-mode](http://orgmode.org) has a nice option which allows you to show week-end days in a different color in your agenda. That means that Saturday and Sunday (when I do not work) are fontified with *org-agenda-date-weekend*. {% endmark %} {% endblock %} But there are other days I do not work, like my vacations or holidays. Therefore, I've wrote a patch to add *`org-agenda-day-face-function'* which is optionally called to determine what should be the face used to fontify a day. [This](http://lists.gnu.org/archive/html/emacs-orgmode/2010-11/msg00542.html) allows me to use the same face for holidays and for week-end days, like for last Thursday which was an holiday in France. That patch has been merged in Org last week.
