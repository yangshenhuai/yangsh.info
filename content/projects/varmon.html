--- title: "VARmon" --- {% filter markdown %} *VARMon* is a text-based tool to monitor DAC 960/1100 based RAID controllers. ## History As the Debian maintainer of *VARMon*, I wrote several patches for it. Dragan Stanvecic, the original author did not want to continue maintenance of *VARMon*, therefore I took over its maintenance in 2004. ## Download You can grab it from the [Git](https://github.com/jd/varmon) repository. {% endfilter %}
