--- title: "libparportled" --- {% filter markdown %} This library is intended to control a LED device plugged on parallel port. A such device is composed of a number of LED (default is 8, maybe more is possible, I don't know) plugged on parallel port. ![LED](/media/images/libparportled/leds.jpg) ## Download You can download from the [Git repository](https://github.com/jd/libparportled). ## Example Here is an example from Giorgio Luparia: [ledcontrol.c](/media/src/libparportled/ledcontrol.c) ## Video Giorgio Luparia made 2 videos of what you can do with libparportled: {% endfilter %}
